Patient-Derived Xenografts (PDX)

The Childhood Cancer Repository has patient-derived xenografts (PDXs) established from neuroblastoma, Wilms tumor, Ewings sarcoma, rhabdomyosarcoma, lymphoma, and actue lymphoblastic leukemia. These PDX models are available for limited distribution to investigators in immediate need of PDX models. We are working to fully characterize and expand the bank of PDXs and we will soon have more xenografts available for open distribution.

To view a complete PDX description (origin, growth conditions, pictures, etc.), click on the PDX name to open a pdf data sheet to view and/or download.
